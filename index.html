How Do Creators Make Money?

ToonScreen offers a revenue sharing system in the form of an AAP or Advertising Affiliate Program which pays creators for every click through on ads appearing with their feature.

Each quarter, we set aside 50% of ToonScreen’s net ad revenue for creators. That revenue divided by the total number of clickthroughs across all ToonScreen platforms, determines the creator valuation. Each creator is then paid the click valuation for every clickthrough their feature has initiated provided a minimum threshold of $30 U.S. dollars is met each quarter.
